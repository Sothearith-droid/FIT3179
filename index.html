CO2 emission and atmospheric concentration

These visualisations will illustrate the amount of CO2 emitted in 2018 by each country, amount of CO2 emitted by each region as well as major countries over the last three centuries and how much the concentration of CO2 in atmosphere is changing over the past six centuries.
